Instrutores

Flavio Henrique Silva
Professor Flavio Henrique Silva is a nice person and is one of the founders of the Bachelor’s in Biotechnology degree at UFSCar - São Carlos. He is always interested to contribute with the undergraduation degree and its students; it’s the second time that he is an instructor of a brazilian team in iGEM. His research field is based on molecular biology applied to the control of sugar cane pests and metagenomics of Amazon rivers and lakes looking for genes with biotechnological applications.
